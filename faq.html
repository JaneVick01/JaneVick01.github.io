FAQ



    1. How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?


  • Go to http://print.hz.nl then you can log in and upload the specific file that you want to print. Next go to any printer at school, use your HZ card and you are ready to print.

2. How can you scan a document a send it to your laptop at HZ?


  • Scan your HZ card, document and the files will be sent to your e-mail or PC.

    • 3. What do you need to do when you are sick / show symptoms of coronavirus?


  • First, contact the teachers/helpdesk at school as soon as possible and stay home. Follow the online lessons on stream so you dont fall behind that much.

    • 4. How can you book a project space in one of the wings?


  • Self Service Portal has a page where after you log in, you can click on Reservations & visitors and you should be able to book a room.

    • 5. What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?


  • You have to park your vehicle at delta parking lot.
