Exalogic

Exalogic is a company founded in 2004 that sells a programme called Oberon on platform Microsoft Windows. Oberon is a complex and modern accounting software. Oberon besides accounting has stock, cash register, hotel reception, CRM, wages, attendance. Programme is best suited for small or medium companies. They work with Visual Basic, C#, Html, SQL.

IT roles:

  • UX engineer
  • Data miner
  • Software Developer
  • E-shop designer
