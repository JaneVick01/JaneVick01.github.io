Accenture


Accenture is a consultancy company with over 500 000 employees, the company is on a larger end of the scale. It includes options for both on-sight and off-sight working. They also claim to have flexible schedules and flexibility with locations, however the long list of leaders and the exhaustive use of meaningless jargon goes a way to indicate they may be more rigid than they claim. The company uses technologies such as Java, Javascript, HTML, and other Sales-specific technologies such as APEX, SOQL, AMPscript and Git.

IT roles:

  • Software Developer
  • UX engineer
  • Security engineer
